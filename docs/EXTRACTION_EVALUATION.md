# RC3 extraction evaluation

Evaluation measures extractor behaviour against bounded fixtures and reviewed
pilot records. It does not certify legal correctness or source-publication
rights.

## Tracks

- **Core 10 synthetic CI:** `publications/pilot/core10_eval.v1.json` provides
  stable regression placeholders without real documents.
- **Core 50 internal pilot:** `publications/pilot/core50.v1.json` guides human
  review coverage for real-corpus work kept out of public releases.

## Metrics to track

- schema validity and domain invariant pass/fail;
- proposition type coverage;
- evidence-span presence and quote bounds;
- critic findings and reconciliation outcomes;
- reviewer edits, rejects, and unresolved uncertainty.

## RC3 guardrails

- Public availability is not reuse permission for evaluation corpora.
- robots.txt is not a licence.
- MCHK remains internal-only for public release.
- DCHK evaluation records must preserve the July 14, 2018 publication caveat.
- RC3 makes no public real release and no prevalence claims.
- Privacy scanning does not justify a complete de-identification claim.
- Student-research letters do not unlock Pages publication.
